Olivia Newton-John

Here's a timeline of Olivia Netwon-John's life

  • 1948 – Born on September 26 in Cambridge, England
  • 1954 – Moves with her family to Melbourne, Australia at the age of 6
  • 1964 – Forms an all-girl group called Sol Four and begins performing on Australian TV
  • 1971 – Releases her first solo album, If Not for You, which brings her international success
  • 1974 – Represents the United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest and finishes fourth
  • 1978 – Stars as Sandy in the hit musical film Grease alongside John Travolta, cementing her as a global icon
  • 1981 – Releases Physical, one of the most successful pop albums of the early '80s; the title track tops the Billboard Hot 100 for 10 weeks
  • 1992 – Diagnosed with breast cancer; becomes a dedicated advocate for cancer awareness and research
  • 2005 – Opens the Olivia Newton-John Cancer Wellness & Research Centre in Melbourne
  • 2012 – Marries John Easterling, founder of Amazon Herb Company, and promotes holistic health approaches
  • 2017 – Announces a recurrence of her cancer and continues to raise funds for cancer support programs
  • 2020 – Launches the Olivia Newton-John Foundation Fund to support plant-based cancer research
  • 2022 – Passes away peacefully at her California ranch on August 8 at the age of 73, surrounded by family and friends

"Olivia inspired us with her kindness, resilience, and unwavering optimism. Through her music, films, and advocacy, she brought light to millions and helped change countless lives."
